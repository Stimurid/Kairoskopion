## Aims and Scope

The journal covers the full range of epistemology and philosophy of science:
- Epistemological problems of scientific knowledge
- Philosophy of science methodology
- Logic and philosophy of language
- Social epistemology
- History and philosophy of science
- Analytic philosophy
- Interdisciplinary studies in cognition

The journal positions itself as a leading Russian-language venue for epistemological inquiry and philosophy of science discourse. It serves as the flagship epistemology journal of the Institute of Philosophy RAS.
