## Aims and Scope

The journal publishes research results in traditional philosophical disciplines: ontology, epistemology, logic, ethics, aesthetics, social philosophy, history of philosophy. It also covers interdisciplinary problems at the intersection of philosophy with natural sciences, literary theory, and visual arts. The journal emphasizes "new forms and language in contemporary philosophy."

Target audience: professional philosophical community. Welcomes contributions from established scholars and promising early-career researchers from Russian and international institutions.
