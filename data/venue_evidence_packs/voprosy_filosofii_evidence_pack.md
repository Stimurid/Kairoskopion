## Aims and Scope

The journal supports the unity of the Russian philosophical community and comprehensive representation of domestic researchers' work. It covers classical philosophical topics and contemporary issues across all branches of philosophy. Sections include Philosophy and Culture/Society, Philosophy and Science, History of Philosophy, Asian Philosophies (Indology and Buddhology), Scientific Life, and Criticism and Bibliography.

The journal emphasizes history of Russian philosophical thought, archival sources, and broad philosophical inquiry.
