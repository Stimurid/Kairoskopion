## Aims and Scope

Logos is a leading Russian-language journal in philosophy, social sciences, humanities, and cultural studies. The journal publishes work on classical philosophical topics (Husserl, Heidegger, Wittgenstein, Gadamer, Derrida, Plato, Spinoza, phenomenology, democracy) and contemporary topics (speculative realism, video game studies, hip-hop, cartography, psychoanalysis). The journal follows a "Westernist tradition" and pursues a flexible editorial policy addressing contemporary Russian social issues and global intellectual trends.
