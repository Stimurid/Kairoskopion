## Selection Logic

### Why these 5 and not others?

**Логос (#1):** The only journal with an existing evidence pack. Strongest STS/continental fit. Regularly publishes technology-themed issues. Established brand in Russian humanities.

**Вопросы философии (#2):** Non-negotiable per spec. AHCI is the highest WoS index among Russian philosophy journals. Monthly publication = more slots. Has demonstrably published AI/digital articles.

**Эпистемология и философия науки (#3):** The epistemology/HPS angle is unique among the Top 5. "AI as cognitive form" maps directly to epistemological framing. Scopus Q1/Q2 with VAK K1 is strong. Chosen over Философия науки и техники (Moscow) because ЭФН has broader scope and stronger indexing metrics.

**Философский журнал (#4):** Completes the IF RAS trio (#2–4). Scopus + ESCI coverage. Multilingual acceptance is a differentiator. Younger journal (2008) with active editorial direction. Chosen over Философские науки because ФЖ has Scopus while ФН does not.

**Цифровой ученый (#5):** Thematic fit is unmatched — "digital scholar" is literally the zone. VAK listed. No Scopus/WoS is a trade-off, but the niche relevance outweighs indexing gap for positioning analysis. Chosen over Социология науки и технологий because ЦУ is more directly philosophical (not sociological STS).
