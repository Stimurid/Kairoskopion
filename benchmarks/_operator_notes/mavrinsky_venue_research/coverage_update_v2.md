## New venues discovered + verified (this update)

### Tier A candidates (Mavrinsky-tribe direct or strong)

**Studia Culturae (SPbGU)** — cluster 4/5/9
- URL: http://iculture.spb.ru + pureportal.spbu.ru/ru/activities/studia-culturae-journal
- Founded: SPbGU, faculty of philosophy
- **Editor-in-Chief: B.G. Sokolov** (Doctor of Philosophical Sciences,
  specialist in aesthetics and philosophy of culture)
- 4 issues/year (Vol 1-62 by 2024; Vol 63-65 in 2025)
- RSCI (РИНЦ) + Ulrichsweb indexed
- Scope: theory and history of culture, philosophy of culture,
  aesthetics, philosophy of art, interdisciplinary humanities
- **Mavrinsky fit verdict: strong_candidate** — continental
  aesthetics + culture-philosophy SPb school; "generous interface
  as aesthetic-political form" fits scope directly
- **Proposed Tier A rank: ~6-8** (joins Артикульт + Худ.журнал in
  Cluster D aesthetics/art-theory pool)

**Шаги / Steps (RANEPA SHAGI)** — cluster 11
- URL: https://steps.ranepa.ru/jour/
- Founded: May 2015
- **Publisher: School of Relevant Humanitarian Research (SHAGI) of
  Institute of Public Sciences RANEPA**
- 4 issues/year, **Scopus-indexed since 2019**
- Scope: ancient culture, oriental studies, comparative linguistics,
  **historical-cultural and cognitive research**, sociolinguistics,
  theoretical folklore
- Mavrinsky fit: **adjacent** — multidisciplinary humanities; not
  direct continental-philosophy but openness to dispositif analysis
  in cognitive-historical register
- **Tier B → Tier A-ready** at rank ~12-14
