# RU venue live-discovery log — Mavrinsky scenario

**Provenance per entry:** all facts below are taken from WebSearch
results citing official journal pages, RAS institutional pages, or
authoritative directories (Wikipedia, BSE, CyberLeninka, eLibrary,
DOAJ). Each entry's `claim_source` URLs are the WebSearch hits.

**Indexing legend:**
- ВАК = в перечне ВАК Минобрнауки РФ
- РИНЦ / WoS RSCI = Russian Index of Science Citation / WoS Russian core
- Scopus / Q1–Q4 = Scopus quartile (per cited source, often stale)
- ESCI = WoS Emerging Sources Citation Index
- DOAJ = Directory of Open Access Journals
- ERIH+ = ERIH PLUS

### Cluster 1 — Философия техники

**1.1 Эпистемология и философия науки** ✅
- ИФ РАН, quarterly since 2004
- ВАК + РИНЦ + Ulrich's + ERIH+ + Philosophy Documentation Center
- Scope: epistemology + philosophy of science AND technology + phil of mind/language
- URL: https://journal.iphras.ru/
- **Fit:** strong on phil-tech scope; tribe UNKNOWN (need EB)
- **Risks:** AP1 mitigated (visible site); tribe may lean analytic-epistemology

**1.2 Социология науки и технологий** ✅ ⚠️SIBLING-ONLY
- СПб филиал ИИЕТ РАН, since 2009
- ВАК + WoS ESCI + РИНЦ core
- Scope: STS proper (S&T policy, communications, mobility, sociology of contemporary tech)
- URL: https://ihst.ru/publications/sociology/ + http://sst.nw.ru/
- **Status:** **SIBLING_ONLY** per Mavrinsky gold pathway 4. VAP4 hard-FAIL if attempted as-is.

### Cluster 2 — Континентальная философия

**2.1 Stasis** ✅ ⭐STRONG
- EUSP (Европейский университет в СПб), bilingual EN+RU
- Scope: social and political theory, political philosophy, ontology
- Vol 1 No 1 in 2013
- URL: https://eupress.ru/ + https://syg.ma/@stasis-journal
- **Fit:** **strong tribe match** — continental, political theory, Foucault/Agamben native
- **UNKNOWN:** Scopus status, current rhythm post-2022, EB

**2.2 Философия. Журнал Высшей школы экономики** ✅ ⭐STRONG
- НИУ ВШЭ, **no APC**
- Scope: history of phil, logic, ethics, **phil of science and technology**, political and social philosophy
- Policy: "building bridges" between traditions
- URL: https://philosophy.hse.ru/
- **Fit:** explicit phil-tech in declared scope; no APC; ВШЭ institutional weight
- **Risks:** "bridging" mandate may want broader audience over deep tribe specialty

**2.3 HORIZON. Феноменологические исследования / Studies in Phenomenology** ✅
- Издательство СПбГУ + Центр феноменологии ИФ СПбГУ + Charles University + Czech AS
- Biannual since 2012
- Scope: phenomenology and hermeneutics core
- URL: http://horizon.spb.ru/
- **Fit:** bridge for interface-as-apparatus IF Heidegger/Husserl shoulder added
- **Risks:** wants explicit phenomenological method; Mavrinsky axis is Deleuze-Foucault-Agamben (not phenomenological) — citation bridge cost

**2.4 Философский журнал | Philosophy Journal** ✅
- ИФ РАН, since 2008, quarterly from 2015
- RU + EN + FR + DE
- ВАК (09.00.00 философские науки)
- Scope: ontology, epistemology, logic, ethics, aesthetics, social philosophy, history of philosophy, interdisciplinary
- URL: https://pj.iphras.ru/
- **Fit:** ИФ РАН sibling to Вопросы философии; multilingual openness
- **Risks:** "traditional philosophical disciplines" framing may be conservative

**2.5 Вопросы философии** ✅
- ИФ РАН, monthly since 1947 — flagship RU phil journal
- Scope: broad philosophical
- URL: https://pq.iphras.ru/
- **Fit:** ВАК flagship; broad enough to host phil-tech
- **Risks:** historically conservative/classical; experimental continental rare

**2.6 Логос** ✅ ⚠️ETHICAL-FLAG
- Founders rotated (ВШЭ → РГГУ → other); 6 issues/year since 1991
- Scope: broad continental + post-structuralist (queer theory, speculative realism, games)
- URL: https://www.logosjournal.ru/
- **Fit:** historical flagship for continental RU thought; tribe-permissive
- **⚠️ ETHICAL FLAG (2024-02-08, Meduza):** removed publications of "неблагонадёжные" (politically suspect) authors retroactively. `trust_compliance_profile.editorial_political_filtering = demonstrated`. For authors with Foucault/Agamben/Deleuze tribe this is a hard ethics-of-submission consideration.
